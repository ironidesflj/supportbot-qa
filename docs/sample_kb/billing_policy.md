# CloudStream Pro Billing Policy

## Refund Policy

Customers of CloudStream Pro subscriptions are eligible for a full refund
within **14 days** of the original purchase date. After the 14-day window,
all sales are final and no refunds will be issued.

To request a refund within the eligible window, contact our billing support
team with your order number and the email associated with your account.

## Subscription Cancellation

You may cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings.
Cancellation prevents renewal but does not trigger a refund automatically.
If you cancel within the 14-day refund window, you may still request a
refund by contacting support.

## Payment Methods

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express),
PayPal, and Apple Pay. All payments are processed securely through our
payment provider.
